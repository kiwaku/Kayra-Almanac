## Power Distribution

- Dual EPS 8-pin to each CPU socket
- 2× 8-pin PCIe to GPU (M40 requires both)
- SATA power daisy-chain for peripheral fans

## Cooling

- Noctua NH-D9 DX-3647 on each CPU (passive mount)
- 3× 120mm intake (DIN rail-mounted)
- GPU passively cooled; case fans provide airflow

## Cable Management

- All cables laced to 2020 extrusion with nylon ties
- High-current runs (EPS, PCIe) bundled separately from data
- Mellanox CX-3 mounted to rear rail with M3 standoffs
