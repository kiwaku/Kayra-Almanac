## Bill of Materials

- **Motherboard:** Dual-socket LGA2011-3 (C612, narrow-ILM)
- **CPU:** 2× Xeon E5-2650 v3 (10C/20T each, 40 threads total)
- **RAM:** 8× 16GB DDR4 ECC RDIMM (128GB total)
- **GPU:** Tesla M40 (24GB VRAM, passive cooling)
- **Networking:** Mellanox ConnectX-3 (InfiniBand/Ethernet)
- **Rails:** DIN + 2020 aluminum extrusion
- **Mounts:** M3 standoffs, lacing bars, cable management clips
- **PSU:** 1200W 80+ Platinum (dual 8-pin EPS, quad PCIe)
